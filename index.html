How to Play

Mines of Life is a unique combination of Minesweeper and Conway's Game of Life.

  • Click on cells to reveal them. Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to the cell.
  • Avoid clicking on mines!
  • Flag cells you suspect contain mines.
  • Every few turns, the grid evolves according to Conway's Game of Life rules.
  • Revealed cells can become hidden again based on the Life rules, so stay on your toes!
  • Your goal is to reveal all non-mined cells without triggering a mine.
